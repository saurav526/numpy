Linear Algebra Functions (One Line Each)

np.dot() – Computes the dot product of two arrays or vectors.

np.matmul() – Performs matrix multiplication between two matrices.

np.linalg.det() – Calculates the determinant of a square matrix.

np.linalg.inv() – Finds the inverse of a square matrix.

np.linalg.eig() – Returns the eigenvalues and eigenvectors of a matrix.

np.linalg.svd() – Decomposes a matrix into U, S, and V matrices.

np.linalg.solve() – Solves a system of linear equations of the form Ax = b.

np.linalg.matrix_rank() – Determines the rank of a matrix.
